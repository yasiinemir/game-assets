Privacy Policy

As Yasin Eren Emir - YEEG ("we", "us", or "Developer"), we value the privacy of Quiz Kingdom ("Service" or "Game") users. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the Service:

  • Identity Information: Your Email address to enable login and secure your account.
  • Profile Information: The Username you choose to be displayed to other players in the game.
  • Technical Data: Device Information, IP Address, and Operating System version for automatic language selection and debugging (logs).
  • Game Statistics: Your scores, win/loss status, trophy count, and in-game progress.

2. Purpose of Data Use

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create user accounts and provide secure login.
  • To perform in-game matchmaking and create leaderboards.
  • To detect and fix technical issues (bugs/errors) in the game.
  • To notify you about important updates regarding the game.

3. Third-Party Services and Sharing

We do not share or sell your personal data to advertising companies. No advertisements are displayed in our game.

However, we use technical infrastructure providers to operate the application:

  • Private Backend Server: Your game data and account are stored in our secure SQL databases.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google): Used solely to send you instant notifications (turn alerts, new matches, etc.).
  • GitHub Pages: Used as a content delivery network to quickly load game visual and audio files (Assets).

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, secure server structures) to protect your data. However, please note that no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is not specifically designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. Parents may contact us to request the deletion of their children's data.

6. User Rights and Contact

Under KVKK and GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request the complete deletion of your data.
